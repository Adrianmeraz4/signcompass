Lesson 4: H-N

4.4 J

True complexity in sign language begins with the letter J, one of the only 2 dynamic alphabet signs

To begin start your hand making the letter I. You can go back to the previous part for reference

You will then begin to make a circle with finger 5. Start by rotating to the right clockwise

When you start reaching the bottom your palm should now be facing you instead of outward where it started

Finally, when you bring your hand back up to complete the circle keep your palm facing you so you are essentially making an I sign to yourself
