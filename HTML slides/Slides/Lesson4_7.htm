Lesson 4: H-N

4.7 M

M and N are very similar symbols with a key difference, the position of finger 1

For M, your palm should face your listener and fingers 2, 3, 4, and 5 will be closed

Now put finger 1 between fingers 4 and 5 going behind the closed fingers

A tip for remembering M from N. Look at the fingers on top of the thumb. If there are 3 its an M and if its 2 its an N
