Lesson 4: H-N

4.8 N

As you can see with the example, N is extremely similar to N

To start, the palm faces the listener and fingers 2, 3, 4, and 5 are closed into the palm

This time finger 1 goes between finger 3, and 4 behind the closed fingers

Again, a tip for remembering M from N. Look at the fingers on top of the thumb. If there are 3 its an M and if its 2 its an N
